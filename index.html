Welcome to Times City Complex

Times City Complex, a sophisticated modern development at 7 Acres of total land area and situated in the Prime Commercial Area of Yangon, including 6,000 sq-m of luxurious business complex in Yangon.

The first glass bridge in Yangon

Times City’s popular Glass Bridge is the connection between two Malls, Luxurious Jewellery Mall and Timeless Times Mall. It enables visitors to experience the thrill of walking on the glass at a height. To prove its safety, a car was driven over it in the Times City Complex opening Ceremony. It’s so strong that they dare you to jump up and down.

Global Buddha Statues

Another famous place in Times City is Global Buddha Statues circulating Banyan Tree, a place where people can go for peaceful tranquility and introspection.
